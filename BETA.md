## Invite Message

> Hi [Name]!
>
> I love walking the Berkeley paths and I hope you do too! There are 121 public paths and stairways throughout the city, and this app helps you find the ones near you and tracks your progress towards walking them all.
>
> The Berkeley Path Wanderers map is a great resource, but it's not very convenient when you're actually out walking. This app is designed to fix that.
>
> You can try it here: [GitHub Pages URL]
>
> **It's designed to be used on your phone.** Add it to your home screen like a native app — the app will walk you through how — then take it out on a walk and see how it goes!
>
> **A few things worth trying:**
> - Install it to your home screen
> - Allow location access to see paths near you
> - Find a path, walk it, and mark it complete
> - Check whether the path lines on the map match reality
>
> I'd really appreciate if you could fill out this short feedback form after trying it: [Form URL]
>
> Thanks so much — your feedback will really help shape where the app goes next.
