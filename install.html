Install Berkeley Paths Navigator

Add the app to your home screen for the best experience.

Why install the app?

  • 📍 Your progress is saved on your device — no account or sign-in needed. Check off paths as you walk them and your history stays put.
  • 📶 Works offline — the app and map load even without cell service, so you can navigate on the trail without a signal.
  • ⚡ Opens instantly from your home screen, just like a native app — no browser, no searching.
  • 🚫 No app store required — install it directly from your browser in seconds.
iPhone / iPad (Safari)
1

Open Safari and navigate to the app. (The install option only appears in Safari — it won't work from Chrome or Firefox on iPhone.)

2

Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen — it looks like this:

3

Scroll down in the share sheet and tap Add to Home Screen.

4

Tap Add in the top-right corner. The app icon will appear on your home screen.

Android (Chrome) — manual fallback
1

When you first open the app in Chrome, an install prompt should appear automatically — just tap Install app to add it to your home screen.

💡 If the prompt didn't appear, follow the steps below to install manually.
2

Open Chrome and navigate to the app. Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner — it looks like this:

3

Tap Add to Home screen from the menu.

4

Tap Add in the dialog that appears. The app icon will be added to your home screen.

Android (Samsung Internet)
1

Open Samsung Internet and navigate to the app.

2

Tap the menu icon (three horizontal lines) at the bottom of the screen.

3

Tap Add page to → Home screen.

4

Tap Add to confirm.
